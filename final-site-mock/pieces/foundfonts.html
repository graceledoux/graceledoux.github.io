Found Fonts

This is a series of posters I created for an Integrative Studio class final, in which I used photos of writing on the street (tags, graffiti, notes, etc.) to create whole alphabets to be used as typefaces in design projects. Each letter was created using lines and shapes from letters in the source grafitti. I wanted to bring to light the fact that all this fascinating, memorable writing is overlooked on the street everyday while designers are always looking for good faux handwriting typefaces.
