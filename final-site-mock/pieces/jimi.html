Jimi Hendrix Un-Illustrated

Project: Design a book based on only the content of a chosen Wikipedia page.

I chose to base my book off the Wiki page on Jimi Hendrix, as a huge fan of his music and of 1960s psychadelic styling. The challenge was to create interesting, dynamic content about this intensely visual icon without the use of pictures. I decided to focus only on Hendrix's life and music, with page numbers in reverse, counting down to his death at the end.
