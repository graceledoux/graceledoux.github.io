Project: Working in teams, design and produce a magazine for a target audience.

My team and I elected to make a magazine for young people interested in finding style inspiration in pop culture sources. This wasn't about cosplay or merch, but instead infusing your day-to-day outfits and surroundings with elements from your favorite media. In my sections, I covered Game of Thrones texture inspiration, Wet Hot American Summer decor ideas, and looking into the details of prints found in 2017's Beauty and the Beast. I also designed the section openers, front and back covers, and directed the magazine.
