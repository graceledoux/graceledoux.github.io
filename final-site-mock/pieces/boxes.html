Boxed Up

Project: Using patterns and shapes found in the debris on the sidewalk, adorn the surface of hand-constructed watercolor paper boxes with original designs.

These are just a few of the 16 boxes I put together in three days, each with an original textural pattern hand-drawn with tiny Micron pens. I wanted to focus on sharp angles, clean lines, and tidy craftsmanship.
