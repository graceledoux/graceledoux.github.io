Memory

Project: After collecting thoughts, dreams, and memories for a week, express the feeling of your week in the material of your choice.

To convey teh swirly uncertainty of memory, I decided to construct shadowboxes from foamcore and paper cuttings. The imagery is all from details of my week that specific week, just little things that are easily forgettable (like a "Thank you for shopping with us!" plastic bag) but stuck around in the back of my mind.
